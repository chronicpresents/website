Our Purpose
What is Chronic Presents?

Chronic Presents is a traveling movement arts showcase dedicated to amplifying the voices, talents, and lived experiences of dancers with chronic illness and accessibility needs through the Movement Arts. By creating inclusive performance spaces, we celebrate resilience, creativity, and self-expression while fostering meaningful connections between artists, audiences, small businesses, and local resource providers. Chronic brings together community engagement and charitable fundraising to build support networks and create lasting impacts.
